Environmental Statistics

Objective:
This individual project aims to analyze and interpret environmental data to understand key trends and challenges in environmental health and sustainability.
Approach:
I will collect data from reputable sources, such as government agencies and environmental organizations, focusing on specific areas like air quality, water resources, and biodiversity. Through this project, I aim to generate insights that can inform local community initiatives and raise awareness about critical environmental issues. The findings will also be used to develop recommendations for policy improvements and sustainable practices.
